Powering Sovereign Finance

Xevolith ($XEV) is a next-gen deflationary token with auto-burn, reward redistribution, and founder allocation mechanisms.

View on BscScan

Features

Auto-Burn

1% of every transaction is permanently burned, reducing total supply and increasing scarcity.

Rewards

1% redistributed to the rewards wallet to fuel ecosystem incentives.

Founder Allocation

1% of each transfer is allocated to the founder wallet to support long-term vision.

Burn Mechanism

Xevolith implements automatic deflation: every transfer sends 1% of the amount to a black hole (zero address), ensuring supply is reduced forever. You can verify burn history directly on BscScan.

Tokenomics

Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 XEV
Circulating: 20,000,000 XEV (2%)
Burn: 1% per transaction
Rewards: 1% to rewards wallet
Founder: 1% to founder wallet
Locked team/reserve: 85%
