Tokenomics

Xevolith (XEV) is designed with a deflationary mechanism:

🔥 1% Burn

1% of every transaction is permanently removed from circulation, reducing supply over time.

🎁 1% Rewards

1% is allocated to a rewards wallet for future community incentives and ecosystem growth.

👤 1% Team Allocation

1% is transferred to the founder wallet for long-term project development and maintenance.
