What is Xevolith?

Xevolith ($XEV) is a next-generation decentralized token designed to restore financial freedom. Built on the Binance Smart Chain, Xevolith combines auto-burn mechanics, fair tokenomics, and long-term lock vaults to build real trust and real value.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 XEV
  • Initial Liquidity: 20,000,000 XEV + 0.1 BNB
  • Auto Burn: 1% of each transaction
  • Rewards Wallet: 1%
  • Founder Fee: 1%

Burn Mechanism

Every transfer of XEV automatically burns 1% of the tokens permanently by sending them to the blackhole (address 0x000...dead), reducing total supply over time.

This deflationary model increases scarcity and value for all holders.
